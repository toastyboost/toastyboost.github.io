ITrace - IOS app for school

iTrace is an iOS app that teaches kids handwriting at school and was created with input of educational experts.
Our app was downloaded via the Volume Purchase Program more than 1 000 000 times by schools around the world. It is used in dozens of school districts and helps teachers to explain handwriting.
Get the app via VPP

What is in there for schools

Handwriting lesson is not so boring anymore. Combine teacher explanation and kids tracing in the app to get children really learning.
Available at a 50% price with Volume Purchase Program for educational institutions for more than 20 app units;
Teachers can check the history of every attempt made by each kid to review their success;
3 style fonts: Zaner-Blosser, HWT (Handwriting Without Tears) and D'Nealian. We checked — that are the most popular methodologies in the USA. Also, you can even customize letters in letter options;
We are strict about privacy — we don’t collect any personal data, we don’t show any ads on our app;
Uppercase and lowercase, cursive and block, letters and numbers — all in one app. There is no need to buy 3 different apps to teach your children handwriting.
