iTrace

The iOS app that really teaches your kids handwriting since 2014.
Your kid likes to spend time with your iPad anyway, lets use that time to teach them handwriting! We designed our app with input from educational professionals and feedback from 100 kids. Both sound and interface help make learning easy and efficient.
Try iTrace for Free

How does it work?

Kids write the same letter several times the only way to develop muscle memory. We adapt the difficulty of letter tracing to each child progress at the time.

What do you get?

Your kid is fully prepared for Kindergarten and will have no issues with grades at school;
You will get some free time for yourself while your kid is learning;
You will know exactly the progress of your child how much time they spend in the app and what kind of mistakes they make;
Teach your children with one app we have unlimited children accounts for all your family;
iTrace maintains a history of every attempt made by each player, so parents or teachers can easily review kids progress.

Letters

Style fonts that schools teach
Zaner-Blosser, HWT (Handwriting Without Tears) and DNealian. We checked that are the most popular methodologies in the USA
One app for all letters and numbers
Uppercase and lowercase, cursive and block, letters and numbers. There is no need to buy 3 different apps to teach your kid just handwriting.
Left and Right handwriting orientation.
Just switch in the Parents Zone. We learned that left-handed people write letters differently: right-handed students pull the pen and left-handed push it before hand.
10 unique prizes for each letter
No other app is that generous. Get rewarded for each practicing and improving.
